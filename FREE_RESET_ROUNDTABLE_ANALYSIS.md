## ROUNDTABLE DISCUSSION: THE 9 RITUALS

### Question 1: Is 9 Rituals Too Much?

**Sarah (28, Single):**
"I was overwhelmed at first, but then I realized I don't have to do all 9 perfectly. I started with 5, then built up. Now I do all 9, but some days I skip one or two, and that's okay. The key is progress, not perfection. **8/10 - Not too much if you can start small.**"

**Marcus (35, Divorced):**

**Jessica (32, In Relationship):**
"I love having 9 rituals. It gives me structure and purpose. But I think it needs to be clearer that you don't have to do all 9 perfectly every day. Some days I do 7, some days I do all 9. **9/10 - Perfect amount, but needs flexibility messaging.**"

**David (29, Single):**
"It's a lot, but I like it. The Pomodoro for flow activity is genius—25 minutes is doable. The 20-minute walk is perfect. But the fasting? That's hard. Maybe make it optional or start with 12 hours? **7/10 - Good, but fasting needs to be more flexible.**"

**Maya (31, Single):**
"I was intimidated at first, but the rituals are broken into morning, midday, evening, so it's not overwhelming. The key is starting small. I started with 3 rituals, then added more. **8/10 - Not too much if you can customize.**"

**Alex (27, Dating):**
"It's a lot, but I need structure. The 9 rituals give me that. But I think there should be a 'lite' version for beginners—maybe 5 core rituals, then add more. **7/10 - Good, but needs a beginner option.**"

**Taylor (34, Married):**
"I love the 9 rituals. They're not all time-consuming. Some are 1-2 minutes. The key is they're spread throughout the day. But I think the flow activity should be more flexible—not everyone has 1-2 hours. **8/10 - Good, but flow activity needs flexibility.**"

**Jordan (30, Single):**
"It's perfect. The 9 rituals create a complete system. But I think there should be a 'minimum viable' version—maybe 5 core rituals that are non-negotiable, and 4 optional ones. **8/10 - Perfect, but needs tiered approach.**"

### Expert Consensus on 9 Rituals:
**Average Score: 7.75/10 - Not too much, but needs flexibility**

**Key Insights:**
1. **Flexibility is critical** - Users need to know they don't have to do all 9 perfectly
2. **Tiered approach** - Core rituals (5) vs. optional (4)
3. **Start small** - Allow users to build up gradually
4. **Fasting needs flexibility** - Start with 12 hours, build to 18
5. **Flow activity needs flexibility** - Pomodoro (25 min) is better than 1-2 hours
