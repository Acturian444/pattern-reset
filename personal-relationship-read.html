Is this therapy?

No. This is a personal written reflection on your story—not therapy, counseling, or clinical care.

When you’re inside a situation, it’s hard to see the full picture. You’re reacting, replaying, or filling gaps with assumptions. Friends are often too close—or they tell you what you want to hear. What helps is telling your story to someone removed from it: not in the drama, not picking sides, reading only what you wrote.

That’s what you get here. I read what you share—what happened, what’s looping, what you want clarity on—and help you name the emotional pattern, blind spot, or turning point you may not be seeing yet. Not a diagnosis. Not generic advice. A specific reflection on your situation so you can understand what’s really going on and what to do next.

If you need licensed mental health support or are in crisis, use a professional or crisis line. This is for clarity from the outside—not a substitute for clinical care.
