About The Show

Conversations once kept behind closed doors — until now.
The Heart Room is a live confessional talk show where strangers share the truths they've never said out loud.

Broadcast on TikTok, it invites people to walk through a virtual red door and speak what's been hidden — stories of heartbreak, shame, grief, and everything we carry but rarely share.

No scripts. No filters. Just truth. Step inside. Let out what you've been holding in. Because what's said here, heals here.
