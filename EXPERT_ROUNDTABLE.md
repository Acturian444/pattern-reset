# Part 1: Project Brief for the Roundtable

## What Pattern Reset™ Is

**Pattern Reset™** (mypatternreset.com) is a digital self-development ecosystem built around a **pattern recognition quiz** and transformation content. The core thesis: people are stuck in **learned emotional loops** (patterns) that developed in childhood—and awareness + structured interruption = freedom.

### Current Offerings

| Product | Type | Price | Status |
|---------|------|-------|--------|
| **My Pattern Quiz** | Free lead magnet | $0 | Live |
| **Pattern Reset Workbook** | Free PDF (post-quiz) | $0 | Live |
| **Let It Out** | Anonymous emotional wall | $0 | Live (noindex) |
| **The Breakup Reset™** | 22-day paid course | $33 | Live (noindex) |

### The Pattern Framework

- **4 Emotional Drivers:** Control, Validation, Avoidance, Fear of Rejection
- **4 Archetypes:** The Anchor, The Catalyst, The Wanderer, The Guardian
- **8 Patterns:** The Fixer, The Perfectionist, The Escaper, The Overthinker, The Pleaser, The Performer, The Guarded One, The Overgiver

### Ideal Customer

- **Primary:** Women 25–45, post-breakup or in healing phase, emotionally intelligent, willing to invest in self-work
- **Secondary:** Anyone stuck in relationship/emotional patterns, drawn to psychology + manifestation
- **Psychographic:** Values self-awareness, reads self-help, follows coaches/therapists online, likely Instagram/TikTok user

### Current Aim & Status

- **Aim:** Launch successfully, grow quiz → email → course funnel, scale to $1M/month
- **Status:** Product built (quiz, workbook, course, Let It Out). Marketing, paid traffic, and scaling systems not yet in place. No paid acquisition, limited organic reach, no clear launch sequence.

### Gaps the Roundtable Will Address
