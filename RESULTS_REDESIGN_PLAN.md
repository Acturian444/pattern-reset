### User Perspective Analysis:
**After taking the quiz, users want to know:**
1. "What pattern did I get and why?"
2. "How does this show up in my actual life?" (ALL areas)
3. "Why is this happening to me?" (neuroscience/psychology)
4. "What is this costing me?" (stakes)
5. "How do I break it?" (solution)
6. "Why should I invest in this?" (value)

### New Results Structure:

1. **Pattern Identity** (Prominent Header)
   - Archetype + Pattern Name + Strength
   - Why they got this (based on answers)

2. **Your Pattern in Action** (Concrete Examples)
   - Real scenarios from their answers
   - How it shows up RIGHT NOW

3. **The Science Behind Your Pattern** (Credibility)
   - Neuroscience: Neural pathways, pattern reinforcement
   - Psychology: Why this developed, how it operates
   - Nervous System: Fight/flight/freeze responses
   - Research citations

4. **How Your Pattern Shows Up Across ALL Life Areas**
   - Love & Relationships (with specific examples)
   - Money & Finances (with specific examples)
   - Health & Habits (with specific examples)
   - Career & Work (with specific examples)
   - Identity & Self-Worth (with specific examples)
   - Childhood Origin (why it developed)
   - Relationship Patterns (intimacy, commitment, etc.)

5. **Why Your Pattern Is Unique to You**
   - Based on their specific answers
   - Their combination of drivers
   - Their age/relationship context

6. **What This Pattern Is Costing You** (Stakes)
   - Relationships: What you're missing
   - Career: Opportunities lost
   - Health: Stress, burnout, habits
   - Money: Financial patterns
   - Identity: Who you could be
   - Time: Energy wasted

7. **The Neuroscience of Breaking Patterns**
   - Why awareness isn't enough
   - Why you need practice
   - Why 22 days works
   - Neural pathway rewiring
