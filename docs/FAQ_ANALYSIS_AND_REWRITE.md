# My Pattern Quiz FAQ — Goal, Shortcomings & Rewrite

## Goal

The FAQ must:

1. **Reduce friction** — Answer objections before they take the quiz
2. **Set accurate expectations** — What they'll get, how long, what it covers
3. **Build trust** — Free, no gimmicks, legitimate
4. **Speak to the audience** — Women in confusing romantic dynamics (situationships, mixed signals, hot/cold)
5. **Support conversion** — Clarify value, remove barriers

---

## Current vs. Reality

| FAQ Item | Current Copy | Reality |
|----------|--------------|---------|
| **What is this quiz** | 38 questions, 10 min, 12 domains (Love, Money, Health, Identity…) | 14 scored questions, ~2 min, relationship-focused only |
| **Pattern types** | 4 archetypes + 8 patterns (Fixer, Pleaser, etc.) | 6 relationship patterns (Hot-and-Cold, Breadcrumb, etc.) + 5 her response patterns |
| **What do I get** | Archetype, pattern strength %, 4 emotional drivers, workbook | Relationship pattern, her response pattern, what's really going on, next step |
| **How do I break it** | Pattern Reset Workbook, interrupt tools | Results include next step; no workbook in current flow |
| **Is it free** | Quiz + Workbook free | Quiz + personalized report free |

---

## Shortcomings

1. **Wildly inaccurate** — 38 vs 14 questions, 10 vs 2 min, wrong result types
2. **Wrong pattern system** — Fixer/Pleaser/archetypes don't exist in current quiz
3. **Workbook overpromise** — Referenced but not in current results
4. **Too broad** — "Love, money, health, identity" when quiz is relationship-dynamic only
5. **Generic "pattern" framing** — Could speak more to their situation (clarity, mixed signals)
6. **Redundant** — Some FAQs overlap; could consolidate
7. **Em dash** — "What's a Pattern — and why" (user prefers fewer dashes)

---

## Rewrite Strategy

1. **Accurate** — 14 questions, ~2 min, relationship patterns
2. **Audience-aligned** — Situationships, mixed signals, clarity
3. **Trim** — Remove or merge low-value items
4. **No workbook** — Unless it's in results; say "personalized report" instead
5. **Pattern types** — List 6 relationship patterns from quiz-data.js
6. **Fewer dashes** — Use commas or rephrase

---

## Rewrite Applied

**Reduced from 8 to 5 FAQs.** Removed or merged: "How does my pattern affect my life," "How did I get this pattern," "How do I actually break my pattern" (workbook ref). Added "Why do I keep ending up in mixed signals again?" to speak to their pain. (Replaced "confusing dynamic" with search-aligned "mixed signals" throughout.)
