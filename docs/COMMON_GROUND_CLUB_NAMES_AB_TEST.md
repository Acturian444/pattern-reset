## Research Summary

### Trends (2024–2025)
- **Day + Activity** — Clear, predictable, ritual-forming (e.g., "trivia night," "run club")
- **Short & punchy** — Memorable, shareable, works on social
- **Theme-based** — Activity + vibe (e.g., "Canvas & Coffee")
- **Inclusivity language** — "Community," "Circle," "Connect" signal belonging

### Culture — Third Place Rituals
- Real-world examples: **church on Sundays**, **trivia night every Wednesday**, **run club every Tuesday**
- Names that feel like **part of life** (routine) outperform **event** names
- "Sunday Supper" is an established concept (progressive dinner clubs)

### Market & Competitors
- **Meetup** — "Social Dinner," "Dinner Party," "40+ Social Dinner with Friends" (activity + social)
- **Sunday Supper Club** — Existing brand for progressive dinners
- **Run clubs** — "[City] Run Club," "Tuesday Run Club" (day or place + activity)
- **Trivia** — "Trivia Night" (activity + time)

### Consumer Behavior
- Adults want **predictability** and **low commitment** to start
- "Club" can feel exclusive or formal; "Social" or activity-only can feel lighter
- **Benefit-first** names (what you get) vs. **activity-first** (what you do)

## A/B Test Variants

### Saturday — Walk + Coffee

| Variant | Name | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **A (Control)** | Saturday Walk Club | Clear, day + activity + "Club" |
| **B** | Saturday Social Walk | "Social" signals connection; lighter than "Club" |
| **C** | Saturday Walk | Minimal — like "run club," "trivia night" |
| **D** | Saturday Morning Walk | Time-specific; sets expectation |

**Recommendation:** Test **B (Saturday Social Walk)** vs. **A** — "Social" aligns with friendship/connection positioning without losing clarity.

---

### Sunday — Dinner

| Variant | Name | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **A (Control)** | Sunday Dinner Club | Clear, formal |
| **B** | Sunday Supper | Cozy, established concept; "supper" = communal, intimate |
| **C** | Sunday Supper Club | Combines B + Club for structure |
| **D** | Sunday Social Dinner | "Social" emphasizes connection |

**Recommendation:** Test **B (Sunday Supper)** vs. **A** — "Supper" feels warmer, more communal; matches strategy doc; differentiates from generic "dinner."

---

### Friday — Music + Movement

| Variant | Name | Rationale |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **A (Control)** | Friday Music Club | Clear, activity-focused |
| **B** | Friday Groove | Fun, casual, implies dance/movement; strategy doc favorite |
| **C** | Friday Music Social | "Social" + activity |
| **D** | Friday Night Groove | Time-specific; "night" = going out vibe |

**Recommendation:** Test **B (Friday Groove)** vs. **A** — "Groove" is more evocative, younger, lifestyle; implies movement without saying "dance."
