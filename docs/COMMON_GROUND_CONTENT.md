## Founder Story (Condensed & Improved)

**Section title:** Why I Started Common Ground

**Copy:**

A few years ago, my life changed in ways I didn't expect.

After a serious accident and a long recovery, I found myself somewhere a lot of adults quietly end up: more isolated than I ever imagined. My routines had changed. Friendships had faded. And I realized something that surprised me:

**Making new friends as an adult is really hard.**

Not because people don't want connection — but because there aren't many places designed for it anymore.

Most social spaces revolve around work, drinking, dating apps, or existing friend groups. If you want to meet new people intentionally — people who are also open to connection — it's surprisingly difficult.

I kept thinking: *There should be a better way to meet people in real life.*

Not networking events. Not dating apps. Not awkward meetups.

Just simple, curated experiences where people show up open to conversation and connection.

That's how Common Ground Social Club started.

Common Ground is a place where strangers come together through shared experiences — walks, coffee, music, conversations — and leave with something rare today: new friendships and real human connection.

It's curated, intentional, and designed to make meeting new people feel natural again.

Because the truth is, most of us are looking for the same thing: more community, more shared experiences, more people we can genuinely connect with.

Common Ground is simply a place to find that again.

## Community Guidelines (Simple, Protective)

**For the checkbox:** "I agree to the [Community Guidelines](#community-guidelines)"

**Community Guidelines:**

Common Ground Social Club is built on respect, safety, and genuine connection. By participating, you agree to:

- **Be respectful** — Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior.
- **Show up present** — Arrive on time, participate in the experience, and engage with others.
- **Respect boundaries** — Everyone's comfort level is different. Honor that.
- **Keep it sober-friendly** — Our events are designed for connection without alcohol. Please respect that space.
- **No solicitation** — This is not a place for business pitches, MLMs, or unsolicited promotions.

We reserve the right to remove anyone who violates these guidelines. By joining, you help us keep Common Ground a safe, welcoming space for everyone.
