# Liam Says It — Product & Funnel Brainstorm

**Document created:** March 2025  
**Purpose:** Master brainstorm for Pattern Quiz, funnel, offer structure, and monetization. For expert roundtable review and A/B testing decisions.

---

## 1. The Real Job of the Quiz

The quiz should feel like a **relationship clarity test**, not a personality quiz.

### What the User Believes They're Discovering

- What is happening with their partner
- Whether they're being played / manipulated / misreading things
- Whether they should stay or leave
- Whether they are the problem

### What the Result Page Should Feel Like

> "This explains what's happening."

Then naturally:

> "Want me to look at your specific situation?"

**That's the monetization moment.**

---

## 2. What NOT to Sell

Do **NOT** sell:

- Red flag checklists
- PDF guides
- Ebooks
- Generic courses

**Why:** Low perceived value for relationship problems. Women in relationship distress want **situational clarity**, not theory. They want someone to say: *"Here's what's actually happening."*

---

## 3. The Actual Product (The Winner)

**Most valuable offer:** Relationship analysis.

### Positioning Names (Test These)

- Relationship Clarity Reading
- Relationship Situation Analysis
- Relationship Clarity Breakdown
- Personal Relationship Insight
- Pattern Insight Session

**Never call it:** advice, coaching, consultation — those feel less valuable.

### What They Submit

- Their story
- Screenshots (if needed)
- Context about the relationship
- Key questions

### What They Receive

- Personalized voice note **or** video breakdown
- Explaining the pattern
- Explaining the man's behavior
- What the dynamic is
- What they should watch for

**Why it works:** You're selling judgment and insight. That's exactly why they DM you. You're productizing what they already ask for.

### Better Option: Group Office Hours

**Example:** Once per week — **Relationship Clarity Live**

- People submit questions beforehand
- You analyze them live

**Benefits:**

- Content
- Value
- Scalability

## 12. Product Structure (3 Tiers)

### Tier 1 — Situation Insight

**Price:** $39–$59

**They submit:** Story, partner behavior, key question

**You send:** 3–5 minute voice note

**Why voice notes:** Feel personal and fast.

### Tier 2 — Relationship Breakdown

**Price:** $79–$149

**They submit:** Same

**You send:** 8–12 minute video breakdown

**Why video:** Feels very premium. You analyze: their pattern, his behavior, the dynamic, what to watch for.

### Tier 3 — Priority Analysis

**Price:** $199–$299

**They get:**

- Priority queue
- Deeper breakdown
- Longer response

**Format:** Still asynchronous (no call). Video analysis responses.

### Why Video/Voice (Not Text)

- Scalable
- Feels premium
- Reusable insights
- Easier boundaries

## 21. Offer Options (Detailed)

### Option 1 — Relationship Situation Analysis (Async)

**They submit:** Story, screenshots, key question

**You send:** Voice memo or short video analysis

**Price:** $79–$129

**Why it works:** Scalable, feels personal, no scheduling, fast turnaround

**Critical:** Voice or video — text feels cheap.

### Option 2 — Live Relationship Breakdown (Call)

**Format:** 30-minute call, you analyze in real time

**Price:** $250–$350

**Do not price low.** Your value is insight. You're closer to consultant or psychic pricing model.

## 22. Capacity & Revenue Potential

### Calls

- 4 calls per day × 30 minutes = 2 hours/day
- 16 calls per week (Mon–Thu)
- At $300 each: **$4,800/week** ≈ $19K/month

### Async Responses

- 5 per day, Mon–Thu = 20 per week
- At $99 each: **$1,980/week**

### Combined

- **$6,780/week** ≈ **$27K/month**
- Working only 4 days

## 23. Scarcity Messaging (Critical)

**Quiz results page should show:**

> "I open a limited number of relationship analysis spots each week."

**Then:**

> **This week's availability**  
> Async analysis: 7 spots left  
> Live breakdown calls: 3 spots left

**Why:** Scarcity massively increases conversions.
