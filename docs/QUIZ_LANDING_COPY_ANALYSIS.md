# Quiz Landing Section Copy — Goal, Shortcomings & Rewrite

## Current Copy

- **Headline:** You Keep Repeating the Same Relationship Pattern—And You're Not Sure Why?
- **Subtitle:** There's a reason. This quiz shows you your pattern.
- **Meta:** 14 questions • Be honest for accurate results
- **CTA:** Start the Free Quiz

---

## Goal

The quiz landing section must:

1. **Stop the scroll** — Catch the attention of women in confusing romantic dynamics (situationships, mixed signals, hot/cold, one-sided, etc.)
2. **Create recognition** — Trigger a "that's me" moment so they see themselves in the copy
3. **Build curiosity** — Make them want to discover their specific pattern
4. **Reduce friction** — Set clear expectations (time, effort) so they feel it's doable
5. **Drive conversion** — Get them to click "Start the Free Quiz"

---

## Shortcomings Against Goal

| # | Shortcoming | Why It Hurts |
|---|-------------|--------------|
| 1 | **"Relationship Pattern" is vague** | The quiz targets situationships, mixed signals, hot/cold. "Relationship pattern" could mean anything. Women in these situations may not recognize themselves. |
| 2 | **No specificity** | Doesn't name the situations: situationships, mixed signals, hot/cold, one-sided. Target audience might scroll past thinking "that's not me." |
| 3 | **"You're Not Sure Why?" may feel off** | Some know *partially* why ("I chase" / "I'm too available") but don't see the full picture. "Not sure why" can feel slightly inaccurate. |
| 4 | **Subtitle is flat and repetitive** | "There's a reason. This quiz shows you your pattern." — Vague, repeats "pattern," doesn't promise clarity or insight. |
| 5 | **"Be honest for accurate results"** | Can feel like a barrier or judgment. "What if I'm not honest?" May create anxiety. |
| 6 | **"14 questions"** | Could feel long. Framing as "~2 min" or "14 quick questions" lowers perceived effort. |
| 7 | **CTA is generic** | "Start the Free Quiz" is fine but doesn't emphasize the benefit (discovery, clarity). |

---

## Rewrite Principles

1. **Name the dynamic** — Situationships, mixed signals, hot/cold so they recognize themselves
2. **Sharpen the pain** — "Repeating" + "confusing" + "can't figure out why" = stronger resonance
3. **Promise clarity** — Not just "your pattern" but "what's really going on" / "why you keep ending up here"
4. **Lower friction** — Time estimate ("~2 min") + non-judgmental honesty cue
5. **CTA with benefit** — Emphasize discovery, not just starting

---

## Rewritten Copy (Recommended)

- **Headline:** You Keep Ending Up in the Same Confusing Dynamic—Situationships, Mixed Signals, Hot-and-Cold—And You Can't Figure Out Why?
- **Subtitle:** There's a reason. This quiz reveals your pattern—and what's really going on.
- **Meta:** 14 questions • ~2 min • Answer honestly for your most accurate results
- **CTA:** Discover Your Pattern — Free Quiz

*(Alternative headline if the above is too long:)*
- **Headline (short):** You Keep Repeating the Same Confusing Dynamic—And You Can't Figure Out Why?
