# Organic SEO Research: What Women Are Searching For

**Document created:** March 2025  
**Purpose:** Map organic search behavior to Pattern Reset content, quiz, and product funnel.

---

## Executive Summary

Women searching about relationships fall into **three main intent buckets**:

1. **Clarity-seeking** — "Why does he…?" "Is he serious?" "What does this mean?"
2. **Definition-seeking** — "What is a situationship?" "What is relationship anxiety?"
3. **Decision-seeking** — "Should I leave?" "Am I in a toxic relationship?" "How to get over a breakup?"

Pattern Reset’s positioning — **"Why does he act like this?"** — aligns directly with the highest-intent clarity-seeking queries. These searches indicate someone in an ambiguous relationship, confused by male behavior, and looking for answers.

## 4. “Why Does He…” Queries (Clarity-Seeking — Core Fit)

These are the **highest-intent** queries for Pattern Reset. Women are in an ambiguous situation and want to understand male behavior.

| Query Type | Example Searches | Content Angle |
|------------|------------------|---------------|
| Why he distances | Why is he distancing himself from me? | Men pull away to process; doesn’t always mean lack of interest |
| Why he keeps coming back | Why does he keep coming back if he doesn’t want a relationship? | Uncertainty, fear of commitment, loneliness relief, jealousy |
| Why he gets mad | Boyfriend gets mad when I ask questions | Trust, past trauma, or not serious |
| Why he’s hot and cold | Why is he hot and cold? | Avoidant attachment, fear of intimacy |
| Why he ghosts | Why did he ghost me? | Low investment, avoidant, or moved on |
| Why he breadcrumbs | Why does he breadcrumb me? | Keeping options open without commitment |

**SEO opportunity:** Create dedicated pages or sections for each “why does he…” pattern. These are long-tail but high-intent.

## 13. Summary: What Women Are Looking For

1. **Language** — “What is X?” so they can name what they’re feeling
2. **Validation** — “Am I overthinking?” “Am I needy or is he unavailable?”
3. **Explanation** — “Why does he…?” to understand male behavior
4. **Clarity** — “Is he serious?” “What does he want?”
5. **Decision support** — “Should I leave?” “Am I in a toxic relationship?”
6. **Recovery** — “How to get over a breakup” (post-exit)

Pattern Reset’s sweet spot: **#2, #3, #4** — validation + explanation + clarity. The quiz and Relationship Clarity Analysis sit at the intersection of “I need to understand what’s going on” and “I need help deciding what to do.”
