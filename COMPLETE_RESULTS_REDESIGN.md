### New Structure (User-Centric):

1. **Pattern Identity** (Prominent)
   - Archetype + Pattern + Strength
   - Why they got this (based on answers)

2. **Your Pattern in Action** (Concrete)
   - Real scenarios from their answers
   - How it shows up RIGHT NOW

3. **The Science Behind Your Pattern** (Credibility)
   - Neuroscience: Neural pathways, pattern reinforcement
   - Psychology: Why it developed, how it operates
   - Nervous System: Fight/flight/freeze responses
   - Research: Habit formation, pattern interruption

4. **How Your Pattern Shows Up Across ALL Life Areas**
   - Love & Relationships (specific examples)
   - Money & Finances (specific examples)
   - Health & Habits (specific examples)
   - Career & Work (specific examples)
   - Identity & Self-Worth (specific examples)
   - Childhood Origin (why it developed)
   - Relationship Patterns (intimacy, commitment)

5. **Why Your Pattern Is Unique to You**
   - Based on their specific answers
   - Their combination of drivers
   - Their age/relationship context

6. **What This Pattern Is Costing You** (Stakes)
   - Relationships: What you're missing
   - Career: Opportunities lost
   - Health: Stress, burnout, habits
   - Money: Financial patterns
   - Identity: Who you could be
   - Time: Energy wasted

7. **The Neuroscience of Breaking Patterns**
   - Why awareness isn't enough
   - Why you need practice
   - Why 22 days works
   - Neural pathway rewiring

8. **The Solution: 22-Day Pattern Reset**
   - Problem → Solution framework
   - What you get
   - Why it works (science-backed)
   - High stakes to invest

### Design Principles:
- Minimal, clean, readable
- Well-distributed sections
- No overwhelming boxes
- Clear hierarchy
- Science-backed credibility throughout
- Personal and unique to them
